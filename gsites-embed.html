01 / Who We Are

A studio built
on craft, community,
and creativity.

Hidden Chest Studios is a Malaysian game development studio delivering award-calibre interactive experiences across PC, mobile, and bespoke brand installations. Founded in 2020, we operate at the intersection of game design, technology, and storytelling.

We've shipped interactive projects for AirAsia and Khazanah Nasional, and our own flagship IP Midwest 90: Rapid City! Which is going to be in early access on Steam. Every project is managed directly by a co-founder. No middlemen, no outsourcing the craft.
