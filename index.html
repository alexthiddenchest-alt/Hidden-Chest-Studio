01 / Who We Are

A studio at the intersection
of games, tech & story.

Hidden Chest is a Malaysian game development studio delivering award-calibre interactive experiences across PC, console, and mobile, and bespoke brand installations. Founded in 2020, we operate at the intersection of game design, technology, and storytelling.

We've shipped interactive projects for AirAsia and Khazanah Nasional, and our own flagship IP — Midwest 90: Rapid City — is in early access on Steam and Itch.io. Every project is managed directly by a co-founder. No middlemen, no overhead.
